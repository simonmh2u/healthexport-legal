Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Health Data Export ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your health data and personal information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Health Data We Access

Health Data Export requests permission to access the following types of health data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Activity Data: Steps, distance, flights climbed, active energy, exercise time
  • Heart Health: Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability, VO2 Max
  • Body Measurements: Weight, body mass index, body fat percentage, height
  • Sleep Data: Sleep analysis, sleep stages, time in bed
  • Nutrition: Dietary energy, macronutrients, micronutrients, water intake
  • Workouts: Workout sessions, workout routes, exercise minutes
  • Mindfulness: Mindfulness sessions
  • Other Metrics: Blood oxygen, respiratory rate, environmental audio exposure, and other health metrics available in HealthKit

How We Use Your Health Data

Your health data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Export Functionality: To read and export your selected health metrics to CSV, JSON, GPX, or TCX file formats
  • Data Visualization: To display charts and summaries within the app
  • Automated Exports: To perform scheduled exports based on your configured automation settings

2. Data Storage and Transmission

Local Storage Only

Your health data NEVER leaves your device. All health data processing happens locally on your iPhone. We do not:

  • Upload your health data to our servers
  • Store your health data in the cloud
  • Transmit your health data over the internet
  • Share your health data with third parties

Exported Files

When you export your health data:

  • Files are saved to your device's local storage in the location you choose
  • You have complete control over where these files are stored and who has access to them
  • If you choose to share exported files (e.g., via email, cloud storage), that transmission is handled by iOS and is your responsibility

3. Data Retention

Health Data Export does not retain your health data. We only store:

  • Export History: Metadata about past exports (date, file name, metrics exported) stored locally in Core Data on your device
  • App Settings: Your preferences, automation configurations, and selected metrics stored locally in UserDefaults
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework

4. Third-Party Services

Apple HealthKit

This app integrates with Apple HealthKit to read your health data. Your use of HealthKit is governed by Apple's privacy policies and terms of service.

Apple StoreKit

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment processing.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your health data:

  • Access Control: You can grant or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
  • Data Deletion: Deleting the app will remove all locally stored settings and export history. Your health data remains in the Health app
  • Export Control: You choose which metrics to export, when to export, and where files are saved

6. Children's Privacy

Health Data Export is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. California Privacy Rights

California residents have the right to request information about the categories of personal information we collect. As stated above, we do not collect, store, or transmit your health data beyond your local device.

9. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • Legal Basis: We process your health data based on your explicit consent (HealthKit permissions)
  • Data Controller: You are the controller of your health data; we are a processor acting on your instructions
  • Right to Erasure: Delete the app to erase all locally stored data
  • Data Portability: Your exported files provide data portability

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • All health data processing uses Apple's secure HealthKit framework
  • Data is stored in encrypted iOS containers (UserDefaults, Core Data)
  • The app uses standard iOS security and sandboxing
